Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish that layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets. It's a comforting and hearty meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

The combination of flavors and textures makes it a beloved favorite among pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Layer with noodles, ricotta cheese, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and topped with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
