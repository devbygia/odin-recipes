Crispy Pork and Peanut Noodles Recipe

Home

Description

This crispy pork and peanut noodles recipe is a delicious and satisfying dish that combines tender pork with a flavorful peanut sauce, served over a bed of noodles.

The crispy pork adds a delightful crunch to each bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 ounces noodles (such as rice noodles or spaghetti)
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced pork with soy sauce and cornstarch. Toss to coat and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated pork and cook until crispy and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the peanut butter, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and sesame oil. Stir until the sauce is smooth and heated through.
  5. Add the cooked noodles and crispy pork back to the skillet. Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce and the pork is evenly distributed.
  6. Serve the crispy pork and peanut noodles hot, garnished with chopped peanuts or green onions if desired. Enjoy!
