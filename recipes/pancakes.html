Pancakes Recipe

Home

Description

These pancakes are fluffy and delicious, perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • lemon wedges, to serve
  • caster sugar, to serve

Steps

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter. This should be similar in consistency to single cream.
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time, or start cooking straight away.
  3. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add a little oil to the pan and wipe it around with kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 minute on each side, or until golden brown, using half a ladle of batter per pancake. Keep them warm in a low oven as you make the rest.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges and caster sugar for squeezing and sprinkling over, or your favorite toppings.
